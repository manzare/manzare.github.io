What do you do with your no longer needed things? My friend Nadja did this, and it’s awesome.

There are so many better ways to get rid of your stuff than throwing them away. Look for clothe-swaps, give-away parties, freecycle groups in your town. If you don’t have them yet, make one. It’s a great fun to refresh your closet or just get rid of stuff when you are moving.
